Build

To just see my resume and connect, see my LinkedIn profile

I am currently Product Manager for US Implementations at Pashi, a YC startup that makes SaaS for factory management. In this role, some of the things I do are act as the Voice of Customer by gathering customer requirements and pain points from Operations executives and translating them into user stories for the UI and Dev teams to ideate, develop and release new features and then working with Sales and Ops to roll them out on for new and existing factory floor customers. By training, I'm an industrial engineer (go Hokies!). I climbed up the ranks at Ford Motor Company as an assembly engineer and after a project automating some assembly documents, my interest in code led me to Ford's analytics division as data engineer creating and managing big data products from factory applications. Around the same time I participated in a Design Thinking workshop at Ford to create products that could solve vehicle launch issues. I led the end-to-end development of a web-app from ideation all the way to MVP which we successfully tested at a Mustang production line launch in an Agile fashion and rolled it out to a company wide IT team that would save the company $5M by allowing factories to launch sooner and hence make more reveneue. Then, desiring a more substantial role in product, I joined Pashi and learned a lot of other great skills in an early stage startup like product QA and testing, copy writing, product documentation, product marketing, and competitor benchmarking. Pashi is now pivoting to an adjacent area where I would be more involved in Operations and wanting to have more impact in a product focused role, I've decided to look at the broader spectrum of opportunities.
